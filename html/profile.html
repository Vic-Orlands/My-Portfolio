    Little information about me ⇾

    I have always loved programming as a result of watching tech-related movies and documentaries as a kid and it translated to my desire to study a related course in school and so I studied Information Technology in the University. Studying this in a public Nigerian University didn't give me the edge nor skill I sought after due to poor educational system and so I went to acquire this skill in a bootcamp during my internship period and so my journey as a Software developer began.

    I am a front end developer highly skilled in html, css and javascript to build web accessible and progressive apps. I'm also skilled with facebook technologies like React for web and React Native for android apps and sometimes switches between tailwind css or bootstrap as my CSS framework. I love building sites and so took up free small scale jobs just to boost my skill and expertise in the field and grow nonetheless.

    I am also a creative writer writing technical articles on medium and Dev.to. I am currently a Contributing Author on logRocket, Strapi, and the AfricaHacks Blogs where I am writing technical articles and concepts. I love playing video games, watching movies and listening to musics when I am not writing or coding.
